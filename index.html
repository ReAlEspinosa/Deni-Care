History

Deni-care was co-founded by Matthew Manson (Managing Director) and Melody Eugenio (Chief Executive Officer and Clinical Lead) in the early 2022, both have the compassion to help other people which result into having vision of creating a harmonious and safe environment to the people who needs help with their daily living and centring our portfolio to our NDIS participant to ensure that they have a quality life that they deserve. With Melody Eugenio’s vast experience as a Registered Nurse for over 10 years working in Australia, New Zealand, UAE and the Philippines, and with Matthew Manson who was an Assistant in Nursing, prior to joining the Australian Military, whom is currently a DVA gold card holder, it seemed to be a natural progression to provide care to the members of the community they have chosen to reside in and care about.
Deni-care’s mission is to provide a high and professional level of care to NDIS participants throughout the greater Deniliquin region, by providing healthcare professionals and portfolio to enhance not only quality of life but also community participation and support.
